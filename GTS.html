    Automation in Legal Practices: Simple Legal

    Businesses require a dynamic approach in order to grow. This is already augmented by automation. The intersection of tech and legal world has produced solutions to minimize the scrutiny time and to efficiently help an organization. This has transformed the legal profession in several ways. At present the applications of Al can be witnessed in:

    • Due diligence : Automation tools to uncover background information which includes contract review, legal research and discovery.
    • Document by automation : Software work on templates to create documents based on input.
    • Intellectual property : Machine learning to analyze large IP portfolios.
    • Billing in digital mode : Billable hours are computed automatically.

    Keeping in line with our company's vision of providing efficient solutions through technology, our team of professionals with technology and legal background are working on producing solutions in legal-tech, one such product is Simple Legal. This tool is to provide automation into end-to-end contract lifecycle management: key-term reminders, contract parsing, generating summaries and contract development cycle. This part of the project allows our team to strive for innovative efficient solutions.
